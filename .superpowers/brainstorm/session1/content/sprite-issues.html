Sprite Differences Found

Comparing Sub-Zero and Kano vs. Liu Kang (reference)

What I found so far

All fighters download from the same source (mortalkombatwarehouse.com), but Sub-Zero and Kano have extra bare-numeric frames (e.g. beinghit/01, beinghit/02) that Liu Kang doesn't have. These extra frames get mixed into animations and mess up frame ordering.

Example: duckjump/ category frame differences

Liu Kang (correct)
duckjump/d01
duckjump/d02
duckjump/d03
duckjump/dt01
duckjump/dt02
duckjump/f01~f08
duckjump/j01~j03
Sub-Zero (wrong)
duckjump/01 ← extra!
duckjump/02 ← extra!
duckjump/03 ← extra!
duckjump/d01
duckjump/d02
duckjump/d03
duckjump/dt01
duckjump/dt02
duckjump/f01~f08
duckjump/j01~j03
